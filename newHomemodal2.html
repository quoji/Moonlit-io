PANTY HERO


Panty Hero (胖次英雄) is the debut release from Pi Studio, a six-member team hailing from Shenzhen China. It's currently vieing for the opprobrious record of greatest amount of AppStore rejections thanks to its crude humor and tongue-in-cheek themes. Play as certain North Korean dictators, kung fu film stars, and unlikely "heroes" who have a penchant for underpants. Play now!






LETHAL PURSUIT


Lethal Pursuit is a project created in the Game Development Capstone at the University of Texas at Austin. Featuring multiplayer combat with 4 choices of playable ships, Lethal Pursuit delivers arcade space 3PS action that incorporates flight and verticality.




EVIL EYES


Evil Eyes was a match-four mobile game project made by myself and three other game professionals. It represented a grittier take on the casual match-N games that were popular in 2013. While the project was abandoned, a live gameplay video as well as music from the game are available.






BORN


Inspired by Copter, Born was created during a 24-hour game jam with two other collaborators at the University of Texas at Austin. Though the project was not functional by deadline, the art and music is all here for your perusal. Its theme earned it a Best Audio award.






FLAPPY HUNT


Flappy Hunt was created with Russell Jahn in an 8 hour app creation challenge using Unity. Russell was also a teammate on Lethal Pursuit at the University of Texas at Austin. For this project, I handled art (repainted Flappy sprites) and audio (a veritable gag soundboard).
